Who We Are

ArtSoul Culture is a Hangzhou-based cultural company serving fashion, beauty, lifestyle, arts and culture through talent and creator representation, branded content, live projects and media partnerships.

The company holds a valid Business Performance Permit in China and is qualified to provide performance and artist-representation services.

We assess project objectives, audience profile, content positioning, commercial terms and delivery risk, then manage the process from partnership planning and rights confirmation through production and final delivery.
